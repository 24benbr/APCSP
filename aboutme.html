About Me

My name is Ben Breber, and I am 16 years old. I am a sophmore at Piedmont High School. I play tennis and basketball for the Highlanders. We were WACC champions this past tennis season going 13-4, and finished third in NCS. I played three singles and was named All-League. In basketball we finished second in WACC, going 16-10 with a rocky start to the season. We lost in the semis of NCS and are looking to come back and win it this upcoming year. I am a huge Warriors fan, and just love sports in general. I live with my mom and dad, and I have two dogs too. I have an older brother and sister, both who just graduated college.
